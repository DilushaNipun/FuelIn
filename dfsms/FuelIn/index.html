Story About Us

FuelIn is a responsible public company established in Sri Lanka, specializing in the distribution and management of fuel supply island-wide. At FuelIn, we strive to offer our customers a seamless fuel ordering and queuing experience. Our innovative online fuel requesting and queue management system has been designed to provide a hassle-free solution for our customers to request fuel based on their allocated quota for their vehicle registration number.

Our system also offers features such as the option to order fuel stock for filling stations, allowing for effective fuel stock management. At FuelIn, we are committed to providing a reliable, secure, and efficient fuel ordering and distribution system for our customers.
